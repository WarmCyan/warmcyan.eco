WarmCyan

Welcome!

Here it is, the beginning of the digital garden I’ve been telling myself for years I would create and put out there for some tiny portion of the world to explore!

There’s not much here yet, this is a space I intend to cultivate and grow over time. It’s not quite a blog, it’s not quite a wiki – it’s a space I can play in and talk about things and share my projects and other stuff I think is cool. Maybe it’ll even provide value to someone other than me someday! It’s my own little place in the IndieWeb, a teenie tiny spot on the giant interwebs that is meant to be slow and cozy and warm. There’s no ads here, no AI recommended articles for you to click on, I’m not selling anything and there are no sponsors. It’s just me, my little raspberry pi, and some digital thoughts, writings, and explorations, and I invite you to peruse them at your leisure!

The main interesting page here right now is The Knoxville Index, a semi disorganized list of neat stuff around my hometown! I think most sane people agree community is pretty important, so if you live around here maybe there’s something on that page that will help you build yours, or at least find something new in Knoxville you didn’t know about!

I also briefly stand on a soap box and talk about decentralization and discuss how you can self-host this website yourself with either zero or lots of self-hosting experience! See Decentralized WarmCyan Website.

I’m trying to do project write-ups and documentation so other people can learn from my mistakes, here’s the Projects archive, and my recent project highlights:

  • Techy: Git auth shell

I’m also gradually posting my favorite recipes on my Recipe list to make them easier to share!

Enjoy!
