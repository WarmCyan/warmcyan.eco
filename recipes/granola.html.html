Granola

#recipe #recipe/handsoff

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 25 mins

Ingredients

(ratio is ~6:1 dry:wet)

Dry

  • oats, 4 cups
  • almond slivers, 1 cup
  • chia seeds, 1/4 cup
  • salt, 1 tsp (little too much?)
  • cinnamon, 2-3 tsp
  • nutmeg, ~1/4-1/2 tsp

Wet

  • maple syrup, 1/2 cup
  • coconut oil (liquid), 1/2 cup
  • vanilla, 2 tsp

Other

  • any sort of dried fruit! (e.g. 1/2 cup raisins)
  • coconut (e.g. 1/2 cup)

Instructions

  1. Add dry ingredients to a bowl, and wet ingredients to another bowl, stir each
  2. Combine wet and dry and stir
  3. Cover a cookie sheet w parchment paper
  4. Pour combo onto sheet and spread
  5. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes
  6. Add other ingredients and bake for 5-8 more minutes
  7. Let cool for 5 or so mins before jarring

Notes

If you use nutmeg, go easy!!! Def not more than .5 tsp
